RESEARCH IDEA SUBMISSION
========================

IDEA TITLE:
[One-line title for your research idea]

PAPER TYPE: [Empirical / Theoretical / Methodological]

RESEARCH QUESTION:
[1-2 sentences. What specific question does this paper answer?]

HYPOTHESIS / MECHANISM:
[2-3 sentences. What is the proposed mechanism? What do you predict and why?
State the expected sign or direction of your main result and what alternative
explanations would predict differently.]

---------- FOR EMPIRICAL PAPERS ----------

IDENTIFICATION STRATEGY:
[2-3 sentences. How will you establish causality? What is your source of
exogenous variation? Name the specific shock, instrument, or natural experiment.
If you don't have one yet, say so — the pipeline will help you find one.]

DATA SOURCES:
[List every dataset you plan to use. Be specific — name the database, the
variables, and the sample period. Example:
- CRSP Monthly Stock File (1970-2025): returns, market cap, share codes
- Compustat Annual (1970-2025): book equity, assets, earnings
- FRED: federal funds rate, term spread]

PROPOSED REGRESSION:
[Write out your main regression equation if possible. Example:
Y_{i,t} = alpha + beta * Shock_{i,t} + gamma * X_{i,t-1} + FE_i + FE_t + epsilon
If you're not sure yet, describe the empirical test in words.]

---------- FOR THEORY PAPERS ----------

MODEL SETUP:
[2-3 sentences. What are the key agents, frictions, or market failures?
What assumptions drive your results? Which assumptions are new vs. standard?]

KEY PREDICTIONS:
[List 2-3 testable predictions or comparative statics that distinguish your
model from existing models. What does your model predict that others cannot?]

RELATION TO EXISTING MODELS:
[2-3 sentences. Which existing models are closest? What friction, agent, or
market structure do you add/change? Why does this modification matter?]

---------- FOR METHODOLOGY PAPERS ----------

THE PROBLEM:
[2-3 sentences. What existing empirical method fails, and in what setting?
What bias, inconsistency, or limitation does your method address?]

PROPOSED METHOD:
[2-3 sentences. What is the new estimator, test, or procedure? What are its
key properties (consistency, efficiency, robustness)?]

DEMONSTRATION:
[2-3 sentences. How will you show the method works? Monte Carlo simulations,
analytical proofs, empirical application to a known setting? What existing
results would change if researchers adopted your method?]
